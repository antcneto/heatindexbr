Provides pre-computed Heat Index (Rothfusz 1990) rasters for the
Brazilian Semiarid region (1,477 municipalities, Res. CONDEL/SUDENE
176/2024) at four synoptic hours (00h, 09h, 15h, 21h local time) and
tools for spatial extraction by municipality name, IBGE code, or
user-supplied shapefile. Data are hosted on Zenodo
(doi:10.5281/zenodo.20942619) and downloaded on first use to a local
cache. Interpolated using External Drift Kriging with altitude and
ERA5-Land covariates. Future versions will include monthly, daily, and
hourly products.
